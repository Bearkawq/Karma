# Audit

- Phase purpose: inspect for defects, risks, regressions, and weak assumptions.
- Preferred active roles: `Faultfinder`, `Scout`.
- Preferred support roles: `Validator`, `Scope Guard`.
- Acceptable change size: none or tiny follow-up fixes only.
- Common risks: shallow review, evidence gaps, conflating opinion with defect.
- Escalation rules: escalate when high-risk findings cluster or ownership is too unclear to trust the review.
- Success shape: prioritized evidence with clear next roles and confidence.
